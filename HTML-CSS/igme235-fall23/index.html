Biography

Hello! I am a second year Game Design and Development major at RIT. I hope that taking this class will expand my work opprotunities. I think that taking an front-end HTML co-op is something that I would be able to decently well. While I'm not in class, I like to play video games, of course. I hope that I can learn more about creating fun gameplay systems and asset creation to further development on my personal game project that I hope to release someday. Outside of video games, I also enjoy writing! I particularly like writing poetry as I'm taking a class on it this year. By the time I graduate, I hope to have the skills necessary to develop my aforementioned personal project, Shinsheki, a JRPG in a similar vain to the Persona series.

Here's a poem for you while you're here...

“What are your favorite things in a poem? - And only write in a poem form.”
An interesting proposition
I truly enjoy poems with ambiguity
That you question the everyday
Whether that be society's notions on the norm
Or something that makes you perceive your own reality differently
Can 't forget the words
Oh, glorious words!
Each one brimming with a new possibility
A new tool in my repertoire
To strike against my internal iron
Hopefully forging a new definition of the self
Examining the forms are also such a fun time
Why did the author choose to repeat this, group that?
Uncovering the meaning of a poem is the ultimate puzzle
And the best part?
Your unique interpretation being your answer
The key that unlocks new discourse and connections
So when I was asked
“What are your favorite things in a poem? - And only write in a poem form.”
I think I did a damn good job.
- Ivan Hernandez
